AI-masters

This is the webpage for AI master students at NTNU 2012-2013. This site contains useful information and resources, free to use by the students. The content on the site is a community effort. I will try to hold it as up-to-date as possible, and update it with more stuff, but if you feel you want something on the site, or wish to share something that might be useful to others, see the contribute section on how to contribute to the site.
